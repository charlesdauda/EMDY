About EDMY

Empowering Youth with Purpose & Spirit

Etherean Mission Dynamic Youth is the youth wing of Etherean Mission, inspired by the word "ethereal," meaning heavenly. It aims to empower young people with purpose, creativity, and spiritual awareness. Rooted in a mission to awaken divine potential, the group encourages dynamic living and conscious expression.

Spiritual Empowerment

Encouraging youth to discover their divine potential and live with purpose and awareness.

Inclusive Community

Welcoming all youth, regardless of background, into a space of unity, creativity, and transformation.

What Drives Us

Empowering youth through unity, purpose, and spiritual growth for a better world

Our Mission

To equip people with life tools, unite humanity, and restore self-dignity, especially within African communities.

Our Vision

To create a unified world where everyone activates and promotes the divine potential in one another

Objectives

To promote spiritual growth, unity of life, and revive Africa’s mystical traditions for human empowerment..

WHAT WE DO AS A CHURCH

Living Out Our Faith

We provide high quality spiritual knowledge, processes and practices for the activation of your innate potentials to enable you be the highest and the best you can possibly be.

We know that each person is a unique being and requires different set of life enhancement tools for their development. In this website, we provide you with an array of products so that you work with what works for you.

We work to promote oneness of life so that humanity will come to the realization that we are just one being. Nature confirms this truth that we all share the same resources of life; we eat from the same bowl and we share the same air. In fact, the air in your nostril may be coming from the nostril of another person who is of different faith, race, creed or culture.

We are passionate about world peace through the realisation of inner peace and we provide the tools for each person to awaken to their full God potential.

Many of these tools are available to everyone free of charge, while some are reserved for members only.

Membership is open to anyone who wants join our Etherean Family and commits to make regular tithing to our cause and vision.

Our Core Values

At Etherean Mission Dynamic Youth, our core values form the foundation of who we are and guide how we serve, grow, and connect with our communities.

Service

We believe in contributing positively to our communities, fostering change through action.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, respect for time, and good principles in all decisions and relationships.

Respect

We value diversity, empathy, and consideration for all individuals and perspectives.

Commitment

We are dedicated to growth in five dimensions of life,spiritual,social,physical,
psychological,financial

Oneness

We foster unity, cohesion, inclusion, and interconnectedness across all communities.

Rooted in Christ, Driven by Purpose

In Christ, we find our strength, identity, and direction. His love fuels our passion, and His truth shapes our journey. As youth, we rise each day with the confidence that we are chosen, called, and equipped to impact the world through His grace.
